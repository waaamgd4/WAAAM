The Story of Our Logo

Our logo is a fusion of two core concepts: The 'Shield' represents Safety & Security, while the 'Lotus' (a symbol of Egypt) represents our nation.

Together, they form 'Secure Egypt' — or SAFE. It's a symbol of our commitment to protecting our community.
