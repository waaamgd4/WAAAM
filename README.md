#  SAFE - Security and Awareness For Everyone

# Project Overview:
Dedicated to educating and empowering individuals and businesses to protect themselves in an increasingly digital environment. As online activity grows in Egypt, so do the risks of cybercrimes such as fraud, identity theft, and cyberbullying. SAFE uses creative graphic design, engaging content, and clear messaging to simplify complex cybersecurity concepts and raise awareness about the importance of safe online practices. It also promotes digital awareness in the AI era, helping people stay informed as technology evolves. Alongside its educational mission, SAFE offers a range of cybersecurity services, including guidance for reporting security vulnerabilities, general security assessments, and cybersecurity awareness training for companies and employees, as well as directing users toward trusted tools and practices that strengthen digital protection. By highlighting key threats and how to respond to them, SAFE encourages a culture of vigilance and digital responsibility.

# Project Objective:
The goal of SAFE is to build a secure and informed digital environment by combining impactful safety education with practical cybersecurity support. The organization aims to:

- Increase understanding of common online threats and how to prevent them.

- Provide clear, accessible information on how to respond to incidents like hacking, fraud, or cyberbullying.

- Highlight resources, national support systems, and law-enforcement channels to help victims take action.

- Foster trust in digital platforms by educating exporters, importers, and businesses about secure online transactions.

- Offer essential cybersecurity services, including general security assessments, guidance for reporting vulnerabilities, awareness training for companies and employees, and support in adopting safe online practices.

# vision:
Building a safe and aware digital Egyptian community that enjoys trust and knowledge to protect its data and identity in the evolving world of the internet.

# Mission:
Offering innovative awareness and simplified creative content to promote digital safety in Egypt, while providing practical cybersecurity guidance and services that help individuals and companies protect their data and digital identity from evolving online threats.

# core values: 
 *Empowerment:*
Knowledge is power. The organization equips people to be their own first line of defense, shifting the narrative from fear to capability and countering the disempowerment linked to surveillance and censorship.

 *Clarity & Simplicity:*
Digital safety can be intimidating. SAFE breaks it down into simple, practical steps—like strong passwords, MFA, phishing awareness, and updates—making security accessible to all.

 *Trust & Transparency:*
Public trust is vital. The oganization shares information openly, bases advice on global best practices, and is transparent about funding and partnerships to overcome skepticism.

 *Collaboration:*
Digital safety is a shared responsibility. SAFE will partner with companies, schools, NGOs, and community leaders, amplifying reach and credibility through a broad coalition.
